**Growth Tactics:**

**Target:** 5,000+ followers by Month 3

**SEO for YouTube:**

**Target:** 1,000+ subscribers by Month 6

## 9. GROWTH CHALLENGES & SOLUTIONS

### Challenge 1: Slow SEO Growth
**Solution:**
- Be patient (SEO takes 3-6 months)
- Focus on quality content
- Build backlinks through partnerships
- Create shareable content

### Challenge 2: Low Social Media Engagement
**Solution:**
- Post consistently
- Use trending formats (Reels, Shorts)
- Engage with followers
- Collaborate with micro-influencers

### Challenge 3: Low Conversion Rate
**Solution:**
- A/B test premium option placement
- Improve value proposition messaging
- Reduce friction in payment process
- Add social proof (testimonials)

### Challenge 4: High Bounce Rate
**Solution:**
- Improve page load speed
- Better content quality
- Clear navigation
- Better mobile experience

### Challenge 5: Competition
**Solution:**
- Focus on unique value (auto-fill + guides)
- Better user experience
- More comprehensive content
- Better SEO strategy
